SONGS OF ARCADIA

Songs of Arcadia is a game about a little pan saving the forest fairies that were stolen using his courage and magical powers of his flute. Both the game and the studio are trying to revive the feeling of 90's gameplay combined with modern technology.

It is being developed using Unreal Engine 4, and is planned to be launched on Steam, and potentially Nintendo Switch.
